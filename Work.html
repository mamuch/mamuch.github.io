About Endurance Films

culture. story. art.

company’s Vision

Endurance Films has been in business since 2013 when it was incorporated by four individuals who met at a half-way house in Nairobi, Kenya. With a desire to create content that entertains and enlightens, Endurance Films has reached beyond its humble beginnings to become a full featured studio capable of handling projects of all sizes. Our Staff is made up of creative individuals who are interested and passionate about making a difference through the art of motion picture. With a healthy track record in Local campaigns, short films, and feature length documentaries, Endurance Films is a brand that continues to push the limits of what an independent film company can do.

The campaigns we have been a part of are challenging. Getting people excited about something today has its share of difficulties, but thats a challenge we have continued to meet and overcome for all organizations who have hired us. Our content created has generated millions of eyeballs in short periods of time. We enjoy working with people who have a mission they are trying to accomplish, and we enjoy being apart of that mission and helping them succeed.

ENDURANCE FILMS, INC. is a film production company whose sole purpose is to create entertainment that inspires.

Since its inception in 2013, ENDURANCE FILMS have created films and marketing services for the industry and independent businesses alike. ENDURANCE FILMS have become a creative multi-faceted media company by producing feature length documentaries, commercials, trailers, videos, short films, and other special venue projects for clients.

Nyibol Magok

Co-founder / Writer / Producer

In the middle of the night, The Protectors of Ulenge and Amani Amani junubi is for me are just some of the masterpieces written by Nyibol. As a writer, poet and an inspired individual with sense of love for African culture, Nyibol has help youths and elders alike achieve their potential in literacy during her years of volunteering as a teacher.

Nyibol has written and produced numerous promotional pieces and commercials. She also travels as a public speaker around east Africa with the film sharing her first-hand experiences in creating it. Nyibol is currently working on her first novel.

Mamuch John

Co-founder / Writer / Director

dedicated cinematographer / photographer with a knowledge of film production and scene composition. Very adoptable at handling a high volume simultaneous tasks with ease. Able to maintain an excellent and professional relationships with a team, directors and financial backers.

He has a degree in computer science and is currently traveling abroad to widen his business connections. He has been campaigning for the youths in South Sudan, he is seeking to open several youth centers to improve more youths development in the film industry.

Bakhita Ireneo

Editorial Manager / Writer

Bakhita is a balanda young girl who in the last decade has been working with the Humanitarian Organizations across South Sudan. At age 18, she attained her first-degree honor in commerce. She has masters and PhD in marketing.

In 2015, she graduated from the oxford university, London institute of English Literature and ever since has been working as a part time writer, columnist, editor and state adviser and at the same writes speech for the president Kiir Mayardit.

Moses Wolf Ruot

Writer / Producer

A native of Gambella, Moses has been a professional photographer since the early 2013. His passion for art matured into a career in filmmaking as a producer, cinematographer, and still photographer. He has produced In the middle of the Night, Traditional cast, God in the Streets of Juba, as well as other notable ad spots, short films, and commercials. He also travels east Africa as a public speaker.
